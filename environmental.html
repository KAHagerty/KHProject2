It would not be easily disprovable to say that climate change
has a clear effect on the mental health of children today.
The fear that the current and/or next generation will not live
their entire lives takes its emotional toll on the youth of today.
"Climate change and related disasters cause anxiety-related
responses as well as chronic and severe mental health disorders."
says an article published by the American Psychiatric Association.

In 1952, the first document on mental health was published.
The Diagnostic and Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders, or DSM
was a small pamphlet containing what was known about mental disorders
at the time. The American Psychiatric Association has published 6
total editions, including the DSM 1, 2, 3, 3R, 4, and most
recently, the DSM-5. 3R was when there were updates made, but not
enough to mandate an entire edition.

There has been speculation as to whether mental health is more a
medical, or social justice issue. According to a book published by the Oxford
University press, many people with some form of personality disorder have
to face a lot of judgement, and misunderstanding due to the stigma around
mental health. Much of this lack of understanding causes a vicious spiral
that can lead people down a path of even more trauma than they already face
due to their illness.

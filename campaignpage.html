Many people are probably asking, "How do I tell if I have a mentaldisorder?"
or "How can I help those who do?". Well, if you're concerned about having
some sort of mental disorder, then click the link in the footer to find a
mental hospital near you, so you can talk with a psychiatrist or other
trained professional, and get diagnosed. If the issue is more urgent,
and you are having suicidal thoughts or actions, then call the hotline
with the link in the footer.
And Finally, if you want to help those with mental disorders, then you can
donate to one of the many charities benifitting mental health. Such as,
Mental Health America, American Foundation for suicide awareness,
Child Mind Institute, and many others.
